Meeting Professor Oak

Ash Ketchum begins his journey in Pallet Town, where he meets the knowledgeable Professor Oak. Professor Oak provides Ash with his first Pokémon, Pikachu, and the essential Pokédex.

Rivalry with Gary

Throughout his journey, Ash frequently encounters his childhood rival, Gary Oak. Their rivalry pushes Ash to become a better trainer and motivates him to overcome numerous challenges.

  • Next

Pokémon League

After traveling through various regions and collecting gym badges, Ash finally reaches the Pokémon League. There, he faces off against some of the toughest trainers he's ever encountered.

  • Next

The Championship

In the final showdown, Ash competes in the Championship match. With determination and the support of his Pokémon, Ash battles fiercely to achieve his dream of becoming a Pokémon Master.
